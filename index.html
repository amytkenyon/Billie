Basic information

Name: Billie
Age: 8.5 in human years
Breed: Shih-Poo

Billie has been in the world since September 2011, she was born in Ipswich, but was thankfully rescued from that dump when she was 8 weeks old. She grew up in Kent, sharing her humans with two other dogs, Bess and Bruno but now rules the roost.
